About Us

Serving Creston for more than 29 years

Our office provides legal expertise to the Creston Valley with a smile and handshake. We to support our local hockey teams, businesses, and work hard to be connected with the community. To do this, we make sure our team is the highest level possible in training and professionalism. This is our team:


Our Notaries


Lorne Mann

Lorne was raised in the Creston Valley and enjoys his time out of the office hiking in the surrounding mountains and finding new fishing holes. He has served on the board for the Society of Notaries Public for over 6 years as an advocate for rural communities. His wife Marianne supports him in his business endeavors and often acts as a document language translator.


Lisa Berry Vander Heide

Lisa works hard in the office, and just as hard outside of it. She is a competetive hockey player, playing locally and throughout the Kootenays in the mixed leagues. Lisa is no stranger to responsibility, having taught in various classroom environments, worked in administrative roles, and served as Superindent of Schools for the Athen's school district in Pennslyvania. She brings her same passion for education to her legal work.
